- East City

With a stunning 25,000 plots spread over 2500 acres and all amenities in place, East City is the largest mega township in the entire country. Well-planned and thoughtfully designed to offer a lifestyle of gracious living, East City is located just 21 km from Secunderabad railway station at Bibinagar, on the Warangal highway.

The twin cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad with their strategic location are experiencing a major real Estate boom.The growth of the Information Technology, Tourism and Hospitality sectors as well as improved infrastructure is attracting a larger number of people to the twin cities .With space in the city limits shrinking and it’s cost soaring astronomically, there is no option left but to move to the outskirts. By investing in East City, you have insured an unpolluted environment leading to a better quality of life.

Changing face of East City

- Road Link

East City with it’s improved connectivity, allows you to enjoy all the benefits and facilities of the twin cities, whist sparing you the pollution, traffic density and human congestion that is the bane of major urban centers. We have offered a few acres of land at East City to the Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation (APSRTC) to establish a bus terminus for the convenience of the residents.

Changing face of East City

- Water Resources

A project for the supply of drinking water from the Krishna river through the existing Madhava Reddy canal to East City, Bibinagar and the surrounding areas has already been sanctioned by the government. This signifies the importance that the East City area is going to acquire in the near future and the growth potential of your investment.

- Features

  • Three Lakh litres capacity overhead water tank.
  • Well planned layouts with parks and markets.
  • Provision for schools, clubs, community and shopping centers.
  • Metro Train Service to commence shortly.
  • 4000 acres and growing.
  • 25,000 house plots in a sanctuary of greenery.
  • Asia's largest township with 208 km of roads.
  • Broad black topped roads with tree lined avenues.

- The future is brighter!

Recent development have made City an even more attractive investment proposition.

  • The A.P State Government has allotted 150 acres of land, in an area abutting East City, to the All India Institute of Medical Sciences-level world class Medical Research and Referral University.
  • The 'Special Purpose Vehicle' initiative proposes to extend the reach of the Multi Modal Transport System (MMTS), which would further improve East City's accessibility.
  • The road to Warangal has been upgraded to National Highway (NH) status. This four lane highway designated as NH 202 provides easy accessibility from East City to the Vijayawada-Vishakapatnam axis and consequently further up the Eastern seaboard.
  • A 100-acre IT Special Economic Zone is coming up in the vicinity, which would accommodate over 100,100 professionals
  • The Andhra Pradesh Housing Board has set up the "Singapore Township" with over 2000 apartments near East City.
  • In its Vision 2020 Master Plan, HUDA has earmarked East City and Ghatkesar for housing over one million people.
  • The proposed 159 km Outer Ring Road will connect East City to over 20 satellite townships as well as Shamirpet, Ghatkesar and Medchal.
  • The State Government has decided to replace the Hyderabad Urban Development Authority (HUDA) with the Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority (HMDA), which will dramatically expand the city limits by an additional 4,655 sq.kms., straddling 54 mandals in 5 districts including areas 10 km beyond East City.
  • The Government has already sanctioned a project for supplying drinking water from the Krishna River to East City, Bibinagar and surrounding areas through the existing Madhava Reddy Canal.

They say time is money, and it is especially true in the case of East City. Because the longer you take to make up your mind, the more money you are losing due to the rapid appreciation of land values here!
